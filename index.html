Founded in 1900 and built on hard work, heart, and community spirit, Shortwood United F.C. has been the beating pulse of Nailsworth football for over a century. From unforgettable cup runs to cold Tuesday nights at Meadow Bank, The Wood has always stood for passion, pride, and proper grassroots football.
