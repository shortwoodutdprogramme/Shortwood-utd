FESTIVE DERBY REPORT

A thrilling holiday clash against Brimscombe & Thrupp

Shortwood United started brightly, pressing high and forcing early errors from Brimscombe. The breakthrough came in the 23rd minute when Smith latched onto a loose ball and fired home.

Brimscombe responded with pressure, but The Wood’s defence held firm until halftime.

Shortwood United F.C. is a proud community club with deep roots in the Nailsworth and Stroud valleys. From the Meadowbank terraces to the clubhouse on a cold winter evening, the Wood has always been built on togetherness, hard work, and the support of local people who care about their football.

Whether you're a lifelong supporter or visiting for the first time, you're part of the Shortwood family. Thank you for backing the boys and keeping our club’s spirit alive.
