About Shortwood United F.C.

Shortwood United Football Club, proudly nicknamed The Wood, was founded in 1900 in Nailsworth, Gloucestershire. The club has a long grassroots heritage, beginning in local Stroud leagues before rising through the Gloucestershire Northern Senior League in the 1970s. By 1982 they had joined the Hellenic League Premier Division, winning the title in 1984–85 and again in 1991–92. Shortwood also enjoyed a spell in the Southern League during the 2010s, before returning to the Hellenic League where they continue to compete today.

Home matches are played at Thrupp Tyres Meadow Bank, which has a capacity of around 2,000. The Wood play in their traditional red and white colours, and are known for their strong community ties, competitive spirit, and distinctive identity.
